1. RISK REGISTER (Top 20 risks)

RISK 1: Payment system failure (HIGH)
- Impact: Revenue stops, users angry
- Probability: Medium (vendors have issues occasionally)
- Mitigation: Dual payment providers (PayPal + Mercado Pago), fallback to manual
- Contingency: SMS-based payment confirmation, send invoice for manual payment

RISK 2: Regulatory shutdown (HIGH)
- Impact: Can't operate in country
- Probability: Low (regulatory approval takes time)
- Mitigation: Consult with lawyers early, stay compliant, maintain relationships
- Contingency: Pivot to adjacent market, restructure as marketplace (not carrier)

RISK 3: Data breach (HIGH)
- Impact: Customer data stolen, GDPR fines, reputation damage
- Probability: Low (but consequences severe)
- Mitigation: Encryption, security audits, penetration testing, insurance
- Contingency: Breach notification plan, customer compensation, PR response

RISK 4: Key person departure (MEDIUM)
- Impact: Loss of critical function
- Probability: Low (but startup risk)
- Mitigation: Documentation, cross-training, equity with vesting
- Contingency: Immediate hiring, interim contractor

RISK 5: Massive churn (MEDIUM)
- Impact: MRR drops, cash crisis
- Probability: Medium (if product issues)
- Mitigation: Product quality, customer success team, retention programs
- Contingency: Discount to win-back, pivot product based on feedback

RISK 6: Competitors undercut pricing (MEDIUM)
- Impact: Lose customers to lower-cost options
- Probability: High (inevitable)
- Mitigation: Not compete on price, compete on value/specialization
- Contingency: Improve product, increase switching costs (integrations, data)

RISK 7: Server outage / Platform down (MEDIUM)
- Impact: Users can't book, payments fail, reputation damage
- Probability: Low (with proper infrastructure)
- Mitigation: Redundancy, monitoring, auto-scaling, backups
- Contingency: Automatic failover, manual override, customer notification

RISK 8: Unable to raise Series A (MEDIUM)
- Impact: Runway runs out, need to shutdown
- Probability: Low (if early traction good)
- Mitigation: Hit metrics, build narrative, prepare pitch deck, network with VCs
- Contingency: Raise via angels, bootstrap (lower burn), raise acquihire

RISK 9: Product-market fit doesn't happen (MEDIUM)
- Impact: Can't grow, burn rate unsustainable
- Probability: Medium (startup risk)
- Mitigation: Iterate fast, listen to customers, be ready to pivot
- Contingency: Pivot to adjacent market, merge with competitor

RISK 10: Fraud/bad actors (MEDIUM)
- Impact: Fake bookings, refund fraud, money loss
- Probability: Medium (payment fraud is common)
- Mitigation: KYC, verification, fraud detection, insurance
- Contingency: Freeze suspicious accounts, investigate, refund victims

RISK 11: Accident/liability (HIGH)
- Impact: Passenger or driver injured, lawsuit, insurance claims
- Probability: Low (but happens in transportation)
- Mitigation: Insurance, screening drivers, safety guidelines, waivers
- Contingency: Insurance covers, legal defense, transparency with regulators

RISK 12: Driver shortage (MEDIUM)
- Impact: Can't fulfill rides, low availability
- Probability: Medium (especially peaks)
- Mitigation: Good driver experience, fair pay, incentives, training
- Contingency: Surge pricing, partner with taxi services, use contractors

RISK 13: Passenger disputes (MEDIUM)
- Impact: Chargebacks, refunds, support burden
- Probability: Medium (common in payment disputes)
- Mitigation: Clear terms, good UX, rating system, dispute process
- Contingency: Mediation, refund policy, learn from patterns

RISK 14: Vendor lock-in (LOW)
- Impact: Stuck with vendor, high switching costs
- Probability: Low (but long-term risk)
- Mitigation: Use standard APIs, avoid proprietary tech, maintain backups
- Contingency: Migrate to alternative vendor if needed (expensive but possible)

RISK 15: Scaling too fast (MEDIUM)
- Impact: Quality suffers, churn increases, costs explode
- Probability: Medium (common startup mistake)
- Mitigation: Deliberate scaling, metrics-driven, hiring quality > speed
- Contingency: Pause growth, fix issues, resume when ready

RISK 16: Market saturation (LOW)
- Impact: Market matures, margins compress
- Probability: Low (Year 5+ risk)
- Mitigation: Build moats, expand geographically, add new verticals
- Contingency: Consolidate market position, look for acquisition

RISK 17: Economic recession (MEDIUM)
- Impact: Customers cut budgets, fewer bookings
- Probability: Medium (macro risk beyond control)
- Mitigation: Diversify customer base, build value-focused tier, cost leadership
- Contingency: Pivot to essential services (airport, medical), cut costs

RISK 18: Technology obsolescence (LOW)
- Impact: Stack becomes outdated, expensive to maintain
- Probability: Low (with modern stack)
- Mitigation: Use modern, maintained technologies, regular updates
- Contingency: Refactor to new stack (expensive, multi-month project)

RISK 19: Loss of key accounts (MEDIUM)
- Impact: MRR drops 30%+, cash crisis
- Probability: Low (if good CS team)
- Mitigation: Diversified customer base, account management, retention programs
- Contingency: Win-back campaigns, lower pricing short-term, find replacement revenue

RISK 20: Cyber attack (MEDIUM)
- Impact: Data compromised, systems down, reputation damage
- Probability: Low (with security measures)
- Mitigation: Firewalls, DDoS protection, security scanning, incident response
- Contingency: Incident response team activated, customer notification, forensics

2. RISK DASHBOARD

Track weekly:
- Payment success rate
- API uptime
- Customer churn rate
- Support ticket volume
- Driver availability
- Chargebacks
- Security incidents

Red alert if:
- Payment success < 95%
- Uptime < 99%
- Churn > 10% monthly
- Chargebacks > 1%
- Any security incident
