2. COMPETITIVE MOATS (Why NEXORS lasts forever)

DATA MOAT:
- Every ride = data point (origin, destination, time, driver, rating)
- More data = better pricing algorithm
- Better pricing = more customers
- Virtuous cycle

NETWORK MOAT:
- More drivers = faster pickups = happier customers
- More customers = more demand = more drivers
- Self-reinforcing network

BRAND MOAT:
- If NEXORS becomes synonym for "trusted executive transportation"
- Brand = switching cost (inertia)

PRODUCT MOAT:
- Specialized per vertical (not generic)
- Deep integrations (API, CRM, accounting software)
- Switching costs = high

OPERATIONAL MOAT:
- Better processes, better team
- Competitors need years to build equivalent capability
